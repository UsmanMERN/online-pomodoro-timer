## 🧠 Why is Context Switching the Enemy?

When a developer is in the "flow state," a simple 5-minute interruption (like checking a message or email) can cost up to **20+ minutes of recovery time**.

By using an **online focus timer**, you create protected time blocks. You commit to 25 or 50 minutes of pure, uninterrupted coding. You pause notifications, close your email tab, and let the timer dictate when it's time to rest your eyes.
